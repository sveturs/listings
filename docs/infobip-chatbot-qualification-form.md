## Additional Details for Bot Configuration

### Bot Purpose
The bot will serve as an automated assistant for the SveTu marketplace platform, providing:
- Order tracking and delivery updates
- Customer support and FAQs
- Product search functionality
- Notifications for sellers and buyers
- Shopping cart reminders
- Promotional messages (only to opted-in users)

### Expected Message Volume
- Initial: 5,000-10,000 messages/month
- Growth target: 50,000+ messages/month within first year

### User Consent Management
- Explicit opt-in during platform registration
- Clear unsubscribe option in bot settings
- Compliance with GDPR and Serbian data protection laws
